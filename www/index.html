Our Purpose

We invest in companies involved in the actual cleaning and protecting of the world's waterways in the belief that if we help those businesses grow and succeed, they will do more of what they do, and enormous environmental benefits will be achieved.

Opportunities

Tamyse Capital is actively seeking investment opportunities. If your company is seeking to grow, or you represent a business raising capital, or if you just want to discuss the latest in solutions to clean the world’s waterways, please contact us.
